What We Do For Ukrainian!

We are doing lot of activities for invasion effected Ukrainian people. Food supply, medicine supply,
cloths for childern, accommodation for who leaves the country etc.

Food For Refugee

We serve food for Ukrainian people outside the border of Ukraine. Like Dry Food, Hot Water, Bread. We have volunteer team in Poland, Moldova, Romania, Slovakia, Belarus and also in Hungary. They are volunterring 24/7.

Medicine For Refugee

The Ukrainian refugee are suffering from many diseases. So they need medicine. Pregnant womens and childerns need many different medicines. We are trying with what we have. So we need more and more medicine.

Warm Cloths

Most of the Ukrainian children leave home with their mother. But they don't have good warm clothes. So they are suffering from various cold-related diseases. What is very dangerous for their growth. So we are trying to give them warm clothes.

Temporal Shelter

Although some refugees can go to their relatives homes,but 65-75 percent of the refugees do not have a place to go. So we are trying to send them to refugee camps in different countries. Also we are keeping the rest of refugees in temporary shelter.
